RPSLS game hints

Bringing the classic playground game online

In case you've been living under a rock (no pun intended), here are the rules.

  • Rock crushes both Scissors and Lizard
  • Scissors cuts Paper and decapitates Lizard
  • Paper smothers Rock and disproves Spock
  • Lizard poisons Spock and eats Paper
  • Spock smashes Scissors and vaporises Rock

Difficulties

  1. Easy: Player has all 5 options at their disposal. If the player wins all 5 games, they are the winner. Player has 3 lives.
  2. Medium: Player has Spock removed from arsenal but CPU still has ability to control Spock.
  3. Hard: Player possesses the tradional Rock, Paper, and Scissors but squares up against the CPU who now boasts having all 5.
